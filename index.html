About Us

Simply put,
We build the world’s finest financial product for founders who are fledgling entrepreneurs. Whether the stage is a pre-seed, seed or even a Series A, is immaterial to our operations; we deal in all its shapes and sizes. Perfectly in line with our vision, we have been consistently building an Investor ecosystem where it not only brings capital but also the experience and expertise of the domain.

FUNDVICE understands that success usually needs time and is a proponent of ‘patient capital’, making conscious long-term investments to help companies reach their full potential and generate fuller returns.

We seek opportunities where we share the confidence in the entrepreneur, where we are excited by the business concept and where we believe there is an attractive investment opportunity. Perhaps and immensely satisfying virtue in our trade is in the beginning of true partnerships with the founders and management teams of our companies, just to ensure that we can have maximum impact on their paths to success. This involvement isn’t limited to just capital provision, it evolves further beyond. We work closely with companies on their business models, implement scale through partnerships and share our experiences in developing world-class brands.
